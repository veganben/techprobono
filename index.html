For Technologists:

TechProBono attempts to match technical people of all disciplines with charities, clubs, the next generation of sporting heros, animal shelters, hospitals, schools, good causes and individuals with a technical need, but no access to the people who can help them.

We are right at the start of this project, but good to go. Sign up here to register as a tech who wants to go pro bono



One of the best things you can do now is to help get this project kicking.

To get started right away, go to our github repository.
If you know what it means to fork this project, do that. Check the backlog. Implement an idea of your own.

If you're not that sort of tech, but have ideas with what we can do to help you give your time, please create a page on the wiki with ideas - it's all point and click stuff!

Designers! Why not upload sketches, designs or improve or comment on other people's contributions?

For Good Causes:

We invite you, the Good Causes, to outline your plans, and to make a case for need. Perhaps you already have a static website and you would like a simple form to allow your guests to get in touch with you more easily.

Maybe you are a small family-run charity with your base firmly in the spare room and you feel that you need a more up-to-date look and feel - perhaps there is a designer out there who likes your cause and would be willing to make you a professionally designed logo.

What ever it is, leave us some contact details and we'll get in touch with you
